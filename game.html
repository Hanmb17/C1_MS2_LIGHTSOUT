Elemental Lumina - How to Play

Free the trapped Lumina by collecting all the Elemental orbs.


Rules:

  • Each level starts with Elemental orbs in a specific pattern.
  • Elemental orbs can only be collected when arranged in the below pattern.
  • Clicking on an orb/space will either collect the orb(s) or create new ones, and this action will affect adjacent spaces.
  • You must collect all the Elemental orbs to complete each level and free the trap Lumina before the time runs out.

How to Play:

  • Click on any space/orb on the grid to either collect the orb(s) or create new one(s).
  • Clicking a space/orb will also trigger changes in adjacent spaces/orbs (up, right, down, and left).
  • If you need assistance, click the "Hint" button. The remaining number of hints is displayed next to the hint button.
